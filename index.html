OSRS Route Converter

Convert a route's real task IDs into manually-tickable custom items, so you can walk through your route before the league goes live.

What This Does

Replaces every taskId in a route with a customItem that has the task's real name as the label. Notes, locations, and section structure stay exactly the same.

Why: real tasks only tick off when you actually complete them in-game. Custom items tick off manually, so you can use a converted route as a walkthrough checklist while testing.

How to Use

1
Paste your route JSON

Export from any compatible tool and paste it into the input.

2
Click Convert

Task names are fetched from OSRSTaskHub automatically.

3
Copy the output

Import back into the plugin or your planner of choice.
